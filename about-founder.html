About the Founder

The founder of GreenishWorld NGO is a passionate environmental advocate dedicated to promoting sustainability and improving community well-being. With years of experience in grassroots initiatives, the author has led multiple projects focused on environmental protection, education, and social development.

Through GreenishWorld NGO, the author continues to inspire change by empowering individuals and communities to take action toward a greener future. Outside of work, the author enjoys research, community engagement, and exploring innovative solutions to global environmental challenges.
