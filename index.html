About Us

Greenish World Empowerment and Health Foundation is a Non-profit organization committed to Promoting Climate-Smart Agriculture for farmers, students and rural youth and supporting sustainable farming practices that enhance productivity, climate resilience, and environmental protection. We are committed to improving lives through impactful initiatives. Established the Greenish World Empire Network—a youth-led community championing sustainable farming practices This project marks the beginning of a global youth movement for climate-resilient agriculture. What's Next? The Greenish World Empire Network will continue to grow—connecting, training, and inspiring youth to lead in climate-smart agriculture. This is just the beginning. We invite all partners, organizations, and youth-led movements to collaborate with us in scaling this impact. Special Thanks To the European Union, Global Youth Mobilization, our facilitators, school partners, and every single youth who believed in this mission—thank you for making this vision a reality.
